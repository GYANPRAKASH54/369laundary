Refund Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

At 369 Laundry, customer satisfaction is our top priority. Please review our policies regarding service cancellations, lost garments, or refund requests below.

1. Booking Cancellations

You may cancel a scheduled valet pickup at any time before the valet is dispatched to your address. Once the valet driver has departed for pickup, cancellations are no longer eligible for a full fee waiver.

2. Service Quality Complaints

If you are unsatisfied with the quality of our washing, dry cleaning, or ironing services, please contact our support desk at support@369laundry.com within 24 hours of delivery. We will evaluate the issue and offer to re-clean your garments free of charge.

3. Damaged or Lost Garments

In the rare event of damage or loss caused directly by our operations, we will conduct an internal review. Approved reimbursement claims are capped at a maximum of 5 times the service fee paid for that specific item, up to a maximum limit of ₹3,000.

4. Refund Processing

Approved monetary refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days and will be credited back to your original online payment method (card, UPI, or wallet).
