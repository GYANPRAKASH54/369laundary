Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

369 Laundry respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, and safe-keep your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, request a valet service, or submit inquiries, we collect information including your name, email address, phone number, physical pickup/delivery address, and transaction logs.

2. How We Use Your Data

We process your personal information to schedule bookings, complete pickups and deliveries, update order tracking, and communicate status updates through automated WhatsApp notifications or email receipts.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share your address and contact information only with our authorized valets for the sole purpose of delivery fulfillment.

4. Data Security

We use standard encryption protocols to protect your personal details during transmission and storage. Access to database logs is limited to authorized personnel to prevent unauthorized disclosures.

5. Contact Information

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or wish to modify/delete your account details, please reach out to us at support@369laundry.com.
